Team Rocket Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) dashboard above displays the most up to date Global statistics of the Corona virus in relation to a multitude of different categories. If you wish to learn more expand the tab below.

  • Case Category Defenitions plus icon
    • CriticalCase: The current total number of people with laboratory confirmation of COVID-19 infection for that day, who have been admitted to the ICU or the ventilator due to high risk to health due to immune system failure and or due to complications in relation to the virus while still being infected.
    • DeathRatio: The ratio between the total recorded deaths and total confirmed cases of COVID-19 individuals in a specified population.
    • NewConfirmed: Total sum of people with laboratory confirmation of COVID-19 infection who have been admitted to the hospital, for that day. Laboratory confirmation is performed at reference laboratories at the respective countries. The test can be either a viral test or an antibody test.
    • NewDeaths: The total sum of people with laboratory confirmation of COVID-19, who died to either the virus or any complications in relation to the virus while still being infected, on that day.
    • NewRecovered: Total sum of people who previously tested positive for COVID-19, and have completely rid of the virus from the system irrespective of the repercussions.
    • TotalDeaths: The overall total sum of people with laboratory confirmation of COVID-19, who died to either the virus or any complications in relation to the virus while still being infected, from the first recorded case of that country.
    • TotalRecovered: Total overall sum of people who previously tested positive for COVID-19, and have completely rid of the virus from the system irrespective of the repercussions, from the first recorded case of that country.
    • TotalConfirmed: Total overall sum of people with laboratory confirmation of COVID-19 infection who have been admitted to the hospital, from the very first recorded case of that country. Laboratory confirmation is performed at reference laboratories at the respective countries. The test can be either a viral test or an antibody test.
    • RecoveryRatio: The ratio between the total Recovered cases irrespective of the repercussions and total confirmed cases of COVID-19 individuals in a specified population.
    • TotalTests: The total number of recorded cases where the infected officially requested a test for the virus, after which they were either advised to stay home and self-isolate or be admitted to the hospital.

The information presented in this site is based on official figures provided by the John Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Centre who have been at the forefront of the international response to COVID-19. It gives the information specific to countries starting from the month of January until the latest updated date, and is also backed by the YATKO Surveillance Centre (US). Datasets, charts and maps will be updated on an on-going basis and in line with newly published data.
